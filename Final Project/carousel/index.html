Ixcatecos

With a total population of 351 people, they are an indigenous community located in the northeast region of Oaxaca, Mexico. The main problems that they face are: Poverty, lack of food, homelessness, injustice, no access to health services and education.

Zapotecos de Ixtlan

It is a community of 320 people, the majority of which lives in the Villa Alta district, with only few of them in the Ixtlan district and Choapan. Not much else is known about this community.

Chochos

It is a micro-region in Oaxaca with 900 people that consists of 17 municipalities in the north of the Mixteca Alta. It is a traditionally agricultural community but proverty, land erosion and droughts have made agriculture a secondary economic activity
